    crypto_polkadot

    Description

    Polkadot enables interoperability among blockchains through its unique relay chain and parachain architecture. Leading parachains include Moonbeam, Astar, Nodle, Acala, and over 30 others, which like Polkadot are built with Substrate.

    This dataset is an index of both the Polkadot Relay Chain and most of its parachains, and is based on an index derived from Colorful Notion's Polkaholic.io, a multichain block explorer for the Polkadot ecosystem.
