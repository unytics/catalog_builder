    crypto_kusama

    Description

    Polkadot enables interoperability among blockchains through its unique relay chain and parachain architecture. Kusama is a canary relay chain of Polkadot with separate parachains, typically used for higher risk and early stage projects, or to test new functionality before being deployed on Polkadot. Leading parachains include Moonriver (paraID 2023), Karura (paraID 2003), and Shiden (paraID 2007) and over 30 others, which like Polkadot+Kusama are built with Substrate. This dataset is an index of both the Kusama Relay Chain and most of its parachains, and is based on an index derived from Colorful Notion's Polkaholic.io, a multichain block explorer for the Polkadot+Kusama ecosystem.
